🛒 Online vs In-Store Shopping Preference Prediction
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes consumer behavior to predict whether a customer prefers online shopping, in-store shopping, or a hybrid approach. The dataset includes demographic, behavioral, and psychological factors that influence modern shopping decisions.

The goal of this project is to apply machine learning techniques to understand key factors affecting shopping preferences.

📂 Dataset Information

The dataset contains information about consumer demographics, digital usage, and shopping habits.

Key Features

Age – Age of the consumer

Gender – Male/Female/Others

City Tier – Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3 cities

Income – Monthly or yearly income

Internet Usage – Hours spent online

Monthly Online Orders – Number of online purchases per month

Store Visits – Frequency of physical store visits

Impulse Buying Score – Consumer impulsiveness in purchases

Brand Loyalty – Loyalty towards specific brands

Payment Trust Score – Trust in online payment systems

Delivery Time Preference – Importance of delivery speed
